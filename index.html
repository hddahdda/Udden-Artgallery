People - Juni Svensson

"People" is a photoseries by Juni Svensson captured between Mars 2020 and December 2021. The series follows the life of Juni aswell as of her family and friends during the what will be called the "corona year" in Sweden.

Slussen - Alf Molinder

"Slussen" are photos captured by Alf Molinder at Slussen between the year 1980-2020.

Colors - Steve Johnson

"Colors" are a exhibition by Steve Johnson consisting of his works between the year 2007-2018.
