Welcome to Udden

Contemporery art gallery founded in 1953.


Udden is named after its location Blockhusudden. It was founded by Majvor Gunhildsdotter who herself was an artist, mostly known for painting seagulls with hats. Udden is funded through state contribution aswell as "Majvors fund".
